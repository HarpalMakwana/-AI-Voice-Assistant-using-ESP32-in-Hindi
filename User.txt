🤖 ESP32 XiaoZhi AI Voice Assistant

पूरा Step-by-Step Setup Guide

🛠️ Step 1 — ESP32 को Breadboard पर लगाएं
ESP32 Dev Board को Breadboard के बीच वाले gap पर लगाएं।
⚡ Step 2 — Power Connection करें
ESP32 Pin	Breadboard
3.3V	Positive Rail (+)
GND	Negative Rail (-)

🔴 Red Wire = 3.3V
⚫ Black Wire = GND

🎤 Step 3 — INMP441 Microphone Connect करें
INMP441 Pin	ESP32 Pin
VDD	3.3V
GND	GND
SD	GPIO32
WS	GPIO25
SCK	GPIO26
L/R	GND
🔊 Step 4 — MAX98357A Amplifier Connect करें
Amplifier Pin	ESP32 Pin
VIN	3.3V
GND	GND
LRC	GPIO33
DIN	GPIO27
BCLK	GPIO14
🔈 Step 5 — Speaker Connect करें
Speaker (+) → SPK+
Speaker (-) → SPK-
🖥️ Step 6 — OLED Display Connect करें
OLED Pin	ESP32 Pin
VCC	3.3V
GND	GND
SCL	GPIO15
SDA	GPIO4
💡 Step 7 — Status LED Connect करें
Connection	ESP32
GPIO18	220Ω Resistor
Resistor	LED Positive (+)
LED Negative (-)	GND
✅ Step 8 — सभी Connections Check करें
सभी wires सही से check करें
कोई loose connection नहीं होना चाहिए
